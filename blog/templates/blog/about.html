About me

I'm Psikey. This is my personal website/blog. And here I will share my thoughts on topics that interest me, leaving a mark on the World Wide Web.
One day my level of English will let me to write publications on it, but until then I will write in Russian.

Which topics I would like to share my thoughts on. (The list will probably grow)

  • Programming
  • Anime Culture
  • Games
  • Music

Programming

My first experience with programming took place at school in computer science class. We studied Pascal and already then I liked programming and algorithmization, but I didn't enjoy programming on the antiquated technology. I think there are quite a lot of modern technology that can be used to teach school students instead of Pascal.
Soon I was trying to learn Java, C, C++, Python.
I decided to focus on learning Python and C++.
Python is currently my favorite programming language. His laconicism and power conquered me. I take great pleasure in developing solutions in Python. I have made many small programs for personal use on it. And now I continue to study Python and various technologies related to it.
In C++, it was nice to do programming, too. It is an interesting and powerful language, but Python remains my priority.

Anime Culture

I am not tryhard Otaku so I don't spend much time on watching anime. For me, animation is one step higher than movies because I think animation has more creative freedom and interesting ideas. In terms of product selection, I approach watching them thoughtfully - I don't like wasting my time on pacifiers, even if they are very popular.

Games

The first video game I played was Will Rock, and I knew right away that video games would stay in my life forever. I wasn't tying myself to any particular genre, so I got to know a lot of different genres in each of which I had a favorite game. The most important thing for me when I play a video game is to enjoy it.
